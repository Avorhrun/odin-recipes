Sandwich

Description

A delicious sandwich perfect to enjoy alone or with friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bun
  • 1 tablespoon Margarine
  • 1/2 cup chopped grilled chicken
  • 4 pieces vegan bacon
  • 3 pieces lettuce
  • 4 pieces sliced cucumber
  • 2 pieces sliced tomato
  • 2 tablespoons sauce
  • 2 tablespoons mustard

Steps

  1. Split the bun and lay it open on a counter, plate, or other reasonably flat and level surface.
  2. Attempt to spread margarine evenly on the inside of both halves of the bun.
  3. Aggressively smear the bottom half of the bun with all of the mustard.
  4. Place lettuce evenly on top of the fresh mustard.
  5. Gently stack the chopped chicken atop the lettuce.
  6. Make a little roof for the chicken with the vegan bacon
  7. Carefully balance the tomatoes and cucumbers on the crispy vegan strips.
  8. Lovingly drizzle with copius amounts sauce.
  9. Place the top of the bun with precision and serve immediately.
