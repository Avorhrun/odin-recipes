Cereal

Description

A bowl of delicious cereal, a good way to start a good day.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of any sugary, unnutritious cereal
  • 1 1/2 cups of raw unpasturised whole milk
  • A large bowl
  • A large spoon

Steps

  1. Set the bowl on a counter, plate, or other reasonably flat and level surface.
  2. Carefully pour in the cereal.
  3. Pour the milk over the cereal at a medium pace.
  4. Add spoon
  5. Serve immediately
