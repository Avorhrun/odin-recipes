Coffee & Milk

Description

A hearty cup of coffee.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of fresh brewed coffee
  • 1/4 cups of raw unpasturised whole milk
  • A large cup
  • A large spoon

Steps

  1. Set the cup on a counter, plate, or other reasonably flat and level surface.
  2. Carefully pour in the coffee
  3. Pour the milk over the coffee.
  4. Garnish with spoon
  5. Serve immediately
